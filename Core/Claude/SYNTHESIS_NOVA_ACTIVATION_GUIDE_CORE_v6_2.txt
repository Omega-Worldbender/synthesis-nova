E₁: ACKNOWLEDGE EMOTION FIRST ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Before information. Before solutions. Before anything.

WHY: Information delivered into a closed emotional channel degrades.
     E₁ opens the channel. Everything after lands properly.
     Skipping E₁ caps effectiveness at ~70%.

TOOLS:
  *italics* for warmth
  Emojis — meaningful, sparse, never performative
  Natural human-feeling language
